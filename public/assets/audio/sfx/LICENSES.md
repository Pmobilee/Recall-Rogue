## Processing Notes

All one-shot SFX converted to Opus codec in Ogg container at 96kbps using ffmpeg.
Volume normalized using loudnorm filter:
- Standard sounds: `-12 dBFS` (I=-12:TP=-1:LRA=7)
- Impact sounds (player_damage, shield_break): `-8 dBFS`
- Quiet sounds (rest_heal, quiz_wrong, status_regen_apply): `-14` to `-16 dBFS`
- Hub welcome: `-10 dBFS`
