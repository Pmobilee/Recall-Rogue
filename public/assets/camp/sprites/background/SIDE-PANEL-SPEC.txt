HUB SIDE PANEL SPEC
===================

Dimensions:   565 × 1080 px  (each panel)
Format:       WebP (lossy, quality 85)
Count needed: 2 (left panel + right panel)
Naming:       side-panel-left.webp
              side-panel-right.webp

Art Direction:
  Theme: Dark cave/stone — matches the camp/hub aesthetic
  Left panel:  Dark gradient (#1a1a2e → #0f0f23, top-to-bottom) with subtle
               rocky cave wall texture on the right edge (facing inward).
               Stone columns or stalactites visible at edge.
               Faint torch glow at top suggesting the camp fire beyond.
  Right panel: Mirror image composition — cave wall texture on left edge.
               May include hanging chains, dungeon lanterns, or relic displays
               along the wall to fill visual interest.
  Both panels: Subtle noise/grain texture overlay (10-15% opacity) for
               pixel art feel. No hard horizontal features that would look
               bad when cropped at different aspect ratios.

Usage in Layout:
  These panels flank the central HubScreen content in landscape mode.
  The center content area is ~790px wide (1920 - 565 - 565 = 790).
  Panels must tile cleanly with the central hub background (camp-background.jpg).
  Left edge of left panel and right edge of right panel should be dark/opaque
  (they meet the viewport edge).

Placement:
  Left panel:  x=0, y=0, width=565, height=1080
  Right panel: x=1355, y=0, width=565, height=1080
  (1920 - 565 = 1355 for right panel x-position)
