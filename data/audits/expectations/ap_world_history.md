## 5. Expected Quality Bar
Strong AP alignment with exam tags. Pool architecture is problematic due to the massive `concept_terms` catch-all.

## 6. Known Risk Areas
1. **`concept_terms` at 297 facts** — A catch-all for nearly half the deck. Distractors drawn from this pool will span 800 years, 7 regions, and wildly different conceptual domains.
2. **`empire_dynasty_names` date-mixing** — "1644-1912" (a date range) appears to be in this pool based on the dump data — a date in a dynasty-names pool is a clear POOL-CONTAM.
3. **Cross-unit distractor contamination** — "Final Solution (Nazi genocide plan)" appearing as a distractor for a medieval India question is CATEGORY-TELL.
4. **SELF-ANSWERING risk** — Some questions about the "Ethiopian Christian kingdom" use the word "red sea" in the question stem and "Indian Ocean trade network" as answer — borderline self-answering through context.
5. **Broken grammar** — `apwh_1_062` question contains "the red sea coastal position" (lowercase "red sea") and unusual phrasing.
