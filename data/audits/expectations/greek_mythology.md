Comprehensive coverage of Greek mythology from cosmogony through the Trojan War and its aftermath. Covers the twelve Olympians, Titans, major heroes (Heracles, Odysseus, Jason, Achilles, Perseus), monsters, Underworld myths, and love stories.

## Risk Areas
1. **hero_names pool (92 facts)** — no synthetics, large range of name lengths. Could produce LENGTH-TELL at mastery 4 with 5 options from very different name lengths.
2. **bracket_numbers pool (4 facts)** — below the minimum-5 threshold. Distractor variety will be very low.
3. **POS-TELL via gendered pronouns** — many questions use "he," "his," "she," "her" which restrict the answer space when distractors include both male and female deities.
4. **SYNONYM-LEAK between Greek and Roman names** — roman_equivalents pool is separate, but questions in deity_names pools sometimes name the Roman equivalent in the question text.
5. **creature_names_short (11 facts, 4 synthetics)** — synthetic distractors are LLM-generated names. Quality of synthetics not verified from source data.
