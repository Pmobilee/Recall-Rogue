# norse_mythology — Expectations

## Intended Scope
Full Norse cosmology from Ginnungagap through Ragnarok. Covers the Æsir and Vanir pantheons, Nine Realms, major heroes (Sigurd, Ragnar Lothbrok), Thor's adventures, runic mysteries, and the Eddaic sources.

## Canonical Source
Wikipedia (individual deity/creature articles). Primary texts are the Prose Edda (Snorri Sturluson) and the Poetic Edda — not explicitly cited but implicitly the source of the mythology.

## Risk Areas
1. **object_names pool (65 facts, no synthetics)** — heterogeneous: contains artifact names (Mjolnir, Gungnir), creature names (Sleipnir), AND numeric answers (8 = Sleipnir's legs). Semantic type mixing is a pool design flaw.
2. **god_names pool (63 facts, no synthetics)** — large size with no length split. Mix of short names (Tyr, Sif) and longer names (Heimdall, Njord) possible.
3. **POS-TELL** — Norse myths feature clearly gendered deities; questions frequently use gendered pronouns that can eliminate options.
4. **No chain themes** — Study Temple chain mechanic non-functional for this deck.
5. **Phonetic complexity** — Eddaic names (Þjazi, Skaði, Iðunn) may cause display issues if rendering lacks Unicode support.
