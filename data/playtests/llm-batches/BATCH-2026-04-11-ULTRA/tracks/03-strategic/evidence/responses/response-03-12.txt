REAL CONSTRAINT: The QP-immune mechanic + Charge AP surcharge = player can play at most 1 Charge per turn with 3 AP. With block cards consuming AP, the offense is extremely limited. This is a GENUINELY PUNISHING state if the player didn't prioritize offensive relics.

DEAD CARDS: Strike (QP) ×2, Heavy Strike (QP). These are 3 of 5 cards in hand. 60% of hand is effectively dead.

SURVIVAL ODDS: 4/10. QP-immunity + low HP = severe pressure.

DESIGN NOTE: The Curriculum's Phase 2 QP immunity is the most mechanically demanding state in the game. Having QP attacks deal 0 damage is brutal and genuinely forces Charge play. But with 3 AP and Charge costing +1 surcharge, a player can only execute ONE charge attack per turn. This creates a legitimate "no good play" scenario for players who haven't built around it. This is flagged as a potential balance concern.
