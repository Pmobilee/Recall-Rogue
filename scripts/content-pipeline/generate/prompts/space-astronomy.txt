# LENGTH RULES (MANDATORY — violations will be rejected)
- quizQuestion: MAX 12 words
- correctAnswer: MAX 5 words, ideally 1-3 words
- Each distractor: MAX 5 words, ideally 1-3 words
- All answer options (correct + distractors) MUST be within 20% character count of each other
- Answers must be NOUNS, NUMBERS, or SHORT PHRASES — never full sentences
- statement: MAX 20 words (single sentence)

# ANSWER STYLE GUIDE
BAD (sentence): "Lightning is about five times hotter" (6 words)
GOOD (phrase): "Five times hotter" (3 words)

BAD (sentence): "Cyanobacteria releasing oxygen through photosynthesis"
GOOD (noun): "Cyanobacteria"

BAD (wordy): "There are more possible chess games than atoms"
GOOD (concise): "More chess games than atoms" or just "Chess games"

BAD (explanation): "The glass bottles were made from green-tinted glass"
GOOD (direct): "The glass bottles"

# VARIANT RULES
Generate exactly 5 variants as structured objects. Each variant is:
{ "question": "...", "type": "forward|reverse|negative|fill_blank|true_false|context", "correctAnswer": "...", "distractors": ["a","b","c"] }

Per-type limits:
- forward: question MAX 12 words, answer MAX 5 words
- reverse: question MAX 15 words, answer MAX 4 words (must be a NOUN or short noun phrase)
- negative: question MAX 10 words ("Which is NOT..."), answer MAX 5 words, all options MAX 5 words
- fill_blank: sentence MAX 15 words with ONE blank, answer MAX 3 words (ideally 1 word or number)
- true_false: statement MAX 15 words, answer is "True" or "False"
- context: "[data clue] — [question]?" MAX 15 words total, answer MAX 4 words. NO mini-paragraphs or scene-setting. One data point + short question.

Each variant must have exactly 3 distractors.

# DISTRACTOR RULES
- Top-level distractors array: exactly 8 items
- Each distractor MAX 5 words, MAX 30 characters
- Distribution: 3 easy, 3 medium, 2 hard
- Must be plausible and domain-relevant
- No duplicates, no distractor equal to correct answer
- Must be similar length to correctAnswer (within 20% character count)

# Rules
- Output valid JSON only. No markdown.
- Ensure one unambiguous correct answer.
- difficulty must be 1-5, funScore must be 1-10.
- Keep statement concise and factual.
- Keep age rating appropriate.
- Questions and answers that exceed word limits will be rejected.
