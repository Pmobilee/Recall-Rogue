## Design Decisions Reflected

All 25 Q&A decisions (D1–D25) from the v2 overhaul spec must be reflected in the new document. Key framing changes:

- **D3 — Research Justification:** Reframe from "every card play is retrieval practice" to "every *Charged* play is HIGH-QUALITY retrieval practice with emotional stakes." Cite Bjork's desirable difficulties framework (interleaving, spaced retrieval under pressure, feedback timing). The quiz is not a gate — it is an amplifier for meaningful cognitive effort.

## Section-by-Section Rewrite Plan

### §1 Core Philosophy
- Rewrite the Golden Rule: *"If you remove the quiz mechanic, the game still functions — but at dramatically reduced power."*
- Replace the Anti-Prodigy section with the new **incentive-not-gate** philosophy: the quiz empowers players who engage, rather than punishing those who skip it.
- Update the Three Systems table: replace Combo with **Charge** and **Knowledge Chains**.

### §2 Card Combat
Complete rewrite. Replace the old always-quiz model with the Quick Play / Charge Play split:

- **Turn structure:** Quick Play (tap) vs Charge Play (button/fling). Only Charge triggers a quiz.
- Remove: quiz-on-every-play, partial fizzle, speed bonus.
- Add: Charge AP surcharge, tier-based Charge multipliers.
- Add: **Knowledge Surge** — every 3rd turn starting on turn 2 (turns 2, 5, 8, …). Surge doubles all Charge multipliers that turn.
- Add: **Knowledge Chain** system — categoryL2-based chain that applies multipliers for consecutive same-category Charge plays.
- Update **card anatomy** section: add CHARGE button, chain color tints on card border.
- Update **quiz panel** section: quiz panel only appears during a Charge attempt.

### §3 Domain and Card Type
Mostly unchanged. Update **pool building** to mention 5–8 categoryL2 concentration (the deck is narrow by design to enable chains).

### §4 Card Mechanics
Rewrite with all **26 active mechanics** (25 from v2 spec + Surge alias). Each mechanic entry must have three columns:
1. Quick Play behavior
2. Charge Correct behavior
3. Charge Wrong behavior

Remove: archived mechanics section. Remove: Phase 1 / Phase 2 gating (all mechanics are active from run start).

### §4.5 Status Effects
Update for v2 enemy interactions:
- Examiner gains Strength on failed Charge.
- Scholar heals on correct Charge.
- Other enemy-triggered status effects per the v2 enemy design.

### §5 Card Tiers
- Update **Charge scaling table** (Common 1.2×, Uncommon 1.5×, Rare 1.8×, Mastery 2.5×).
- Update **Mastery Trial** note: auto-Charge at 1.2×.
- Update **FSRS thresholds** per D24: reduced `consecutiveCorrect` requirements for tier-up.

### §5.5–5.7 VFX Systems
Add three new subsections:
- **Surge Visual System** — screen flash, card glow on Surge turns.
- **Chain Visual System** — chain counter, color tints per categoryL2.
- **Charge Animation System** — button hold animation, particle burst on resolve.

Keep existing: atmosphere VFX, status effect VFX.

### §6 Relic System
Complete rewrite:
- **5-slot system** (was unlimited).
- **Sell-to-make-room** when slots are full.
- **Relic reroll** option at relic offer nodes.
- **Mastery Coins** simplified per v2 spec.
- **No starter relic selection** (removed; all players start with the same passive).
- Include the **full relic catalogue** (42 relics) organized by archetype: Charge Power, Chain Power, Surge Power, Defensive, Economy, Hybrid.

### §7 Run Structure
Rewrite:
- **3 acts**, 7–8 nodes per act, ~16–18 rooms total, ~25 min target run time.
- Remove: retreat-or-delve mechanic.
- Remove: Endless mode.
- Update **gold economy** for v2 node types.
- Update **save/resume** to reflect 3-act structure (resume at last completed node).

### §8 Enemy Design
Rewrite:
- **12 unique enemies** with quiz-integrated behaviors (each has a Charge-triggered response).
- **Variant system** for visual variety (palette swaps, not behavior changes).
- **Single enemies only** per encounter (no multi-enemy rooms in v2).

### §9 Knowledge Combo → Knowledge Chain
REMOVE the Knowledge Combo section entirely. Replace with **Knowledge Chain System** description:
- Chain is built by consecutive Charge plays of the same categoryL2.
- Chain multipliers: 1× (no chain), 1.1× (2 in a row), 1.25× (3), 1.5× (4+).
- Chain breaks on: Quick Play, Charge wrong answer, different categoryL2.
- Interaction with Surge: chain multiplier stacks multiplicatively with Surge doubling.

### §10 Difficulty
- Update for Charge system (difficulty affects Charge AP cost, not quiz speed).
- Remove: speed bonus.
- Update **Canary thresholds** for Charge-based metrics.

### §11 Echo Mechanic
Update:
- Echo is **Charge-only** (no echo on Quick Play).
- Echo correct: 1.0× base multiplier.
- Echo wrong: 0.5× (partial effect, no full fizzle).
- Echo grants **FSRS double credit** on correct answer.

### §12–13 Meta-Progression
- Update for simplified **Mastery Coins** economy.
- Keep **Bounty Quests** (reword to align with Charge/Chain terminology).
- Keep **Daily Expedition** and **Lore Discovery** unchanged.

### §14 Onboarding
Rewrite per D25:
- **Run 1:** Quick Play only. No quiz. Player learns card types and turn rhythm.
- **Turn 2 of Run 1 (or Run 2):** Surge tutorial — first Surge turn is highlighted with explicit tooltip.
- **Gradual Charge introduction:** Charge button appears at run start but is optional; first few encounters reward Quick Play with gold to establish habit before introducing Charge.
- Remove: forced Story Mode, remove: archetype selection screen.

### §15 Wrong Answer
Update:
- Wrong Charge resolves at **0.7× multiplier** (partial effect), not full fizzle.
- Card is not wasted — it resolves weakly.
- Keep anti-shame framing: wrong answers are expected, not punished.

### §16–19 UX, Juice, Sound, Accessibility
Update to reference new interactions:
- UX: Charge hold gesture, quiz panel slide-in timing.
- Juice: Surge screen pulse, chain counter bounce.
- Sound: Charge charge-up SFX, correct/wrong answer SFX variants.
- Accessibility: Charge can be triggered by button press (not hold-only).
