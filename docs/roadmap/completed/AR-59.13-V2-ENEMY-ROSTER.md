# AR-59.13 — v2 Enemy Roster

## Overview

**Goal:** Replace the current mixed roster (9 commons, 2 elites, 6 mini-bosses, 6 bosses spread across 4 regions) with a focused 12-unique-behavior roster across 3 acts. Map the 88 existing sprites to these behaviors as visual variants. All enemies are single encounters (no multi-enemy rooms).

**Dependencies:**
- AR-59.5 (Three-Act Run Structure) — act structure and floor ranges must exist before enemy pool assignment
- AR-59.1 (Quick Play / Charge System) — `playMode` field on card play events required for quiz-reactive enemies (Examiner, Scholar, Librarian)
- AR-59.3 (Knowledge Chain System) — `chainMultiplier` field in `TurnState` required for Nullifier's override
- AR-59.7 (Boss Quiz Phases) — `quizPhases` infrastructure required for Archivist and Curator definitions

**Estimated complexity:** High — new fields on `EnemyTemplate` and `EnemyInstance`, 12 behavior callbacks, sprite manifest, variant definitions, act pool config, and `enemyManager.ts` integration.

---

## Design Decisions

| # | Decision |
|---|----------|
| D17 | Keep all 88 existing sprites. 8-10 unique enemies per act; remaining sprites are stat variants (same behavior, different sprite/name, ±10-15% HP/damage). |
| D18 | Forced-Charge encounters (The Examiner, The Librarian) are Act 2+ only. Players must have seen the Charge mechanic before facing enemies that punish not using it. |
| D19 | "Weakest domain" for The Curator = the `categoryL2` label with the lowest `correct/total` ratio across all facts answered during the current run. Tracked in `RunState.categoryL2Accuracy`. If no facts answered (impossible in Act 3 but defensively handled), fall back to the `categoryL2` with the most facts in the run pool. This matches the definition in AR-59.7. |

## v2 Enemy Roster — 12 Unique Behaviors

### Act 1: The Shallows (Floors 1-4)

Enemy pool philosophy: Telegraphed, forgiving. Teach Quick Play, introduce Charge as optional upside. No punishment for wrong Charge (except the existing 0.6× multiplier from AR-59.1).

| Enemy | Category | Base HP | Intent Damage | Special Behavior |
|-------|----------|---------|---------------|-----------------|
| Cave Bat | Common | 19 | 8-11 | Standard attack/buff. Acts as tutorial encounter. |
| Crystal Golem | Common | 38 | 12 (alt: 25 charged) | Defends on off-turns; charges for 25 dmg spike every 4th turn. |
| Toxic Spore | Common | 15 | 8 + poison | Very low HP. Applies poison DoT. Must kill fast or suffer DoT stacks. |
| Timer Wyrm | Mini-Boss | 45 | 12 base | Enrages turn 4: gains +5 damage permanently per subsequent turn. Must be killed fast. |

**Act 1 act boss:** Timer Wyrm (mini-boss gate at end of Act 1).

---

### Act 2: The Depths (Floors 5-8)

Enemy pool philosophy: Introduce quiz-reactive behaviors. Players who ignore Charge face growing consequences. Bone Collector and Shadow Mimic punish wrong Charges specifically, teaching intentionality.

| Enemy | Category | Base HP | Intent Damage | Special Behavior |
|-------|----------|---------|---------------|-----------------|
| Shadow Mimic | Common | 30 | 8 | `onPlayerChargeWrong`: copies the played card's base damage and deals it back to the player (once per trigger). |
| Bone Collector | Common | 35 | 10 | `onPlayerChargeWrong`: heals 5 HP. Synergizes with its existing heal intent — punishes reckless Charging. |
| The Examiner | Elite | 55 | 10 | `onPlayerNoCharge`: gains +3 Strength (stacks, permanent until defeated) each player turn that contains no Charge play. Forces the player to Charge at least once per turn. |
| The Archivist | Boss | 80 | 12 | Boss Quiz Phase at 50% HP (5 questions). Full spec in AR-59.7. |

**Act 2 act boss:** The Archivist.

---

### Act 3: The Archive (Floors 9-12)

Enemy pool philosophy: All mechanics tested simultaneously. Scholar punishes correct answers (forces decision-making). Nullifier shuts off chain economy. Librarian blocks Quick Play entirely. Curator is the final exam.

| Enemy | Category | Base HP | Intent Damage | Special Behavior |
|-------|----------|---------|---------------|-----------------|
| The Scholar | Common | 40 | 6 | `onPlayerChargeCorrect`: heals 5 HP. Creates genuine dilemma — do you Quick Play to deny healing, or Charge for the damage multiplier knowing it heals the enemy? |
| The Nullifier | Elite | 70 | 14 | `chainMultiplierOverride: 1.0` — all Knowledge Chain multipliers are forced to 1.0× while this enemy is alive. Normal Charge multipliers are unaffected; only chain stacking is neutralized. |
| The Librarian | Elite | 65 | 12 | `quickPlayImmune: true` — Quick Play cards deal 0 damage. Only Charged plays (correct or wrong) deal damage. Teaches Charge discipline under duress. |
| The Curator | Boss | 120 | 15 | Boss Quiz Phases at 66% HP and 33% HP. Phase 2 (33%) is RAPID FIRE mode (8 questions, 4s timer). Full spec in AR-59.7. |

**Act 3 act boss:** The Curator (final boss of the run).

## Quiz-Integrated Enemy Behaviors — New Fields Required

### New fields on `EnemyTemplate` (`src/data/enemies.ts`)

```ts
/**
 * Called after a player Charge play resolves as WRONG.
 * Receives a partial combat state snapshot for computing effect values.
 */
onPlayerChargeWrong?: (ctx: EnemyReactContext) => void;

/**
 * Called at the end of a player's turn if the player made NO Charge plays that turn.
 * Fires once per turn, regardless of how many Quick Plays were made.
 */
onPlayerNoCharge?: (ctx: EnemyReactContext) => void;

/**
 * Called after a player Charge play resolves as CORRECT.
 */
onPlayerChargeCorrect?: (ctx: EnemyReactContext) => void;

/**
 * If set, forces all Knowledge Chain multipliers to this value while this enemy is alive.
 * Does not affect Charge multipliers. Used by The Nullifier.
 */
chainMultiplierOverride?: number;

/**
 * If true, Quick Play card plays deal 0 damage to this enemy.
 * Charge plays (correct or wrong) deal normal damage.
 * Used by The Librarian.
 */
quickPlayImmune?: boolean;

/**
 * Boss Quiz Phase configurations. Combat pauses at each HP threshold.
 * Full spec in AR-59.7. Handled by the quiz phase service (AR-59.7 scope).
 */
quizPhases?: QuizPhaseConfig[];
```

### New `EnemyReactContext` interface (`src/data/enemies.ts`)

```ts
/**
 * Minimal snapshot passed to enemy reaction callbacks.
 * Enemy manager reads this; callbacks may mutate enemy instance fields via the ref.
 */
export interface EnemyReactContext {
  /** The live enemy instance (mutable). */
  enemy: EnemyInstance;
  /** Base damage value of the card just played (before multipliers). */
  cardBaseDamage: number;
  /** The playMode used: 'quick' | 'charge'. */
  playMode: 'quick' | 'charge';
  /** Whether the Charge quiz was answered correctly (false if quick play). */
  chargeCorrect: boolean;
}
```

### New field on `EnemyInstance` (`src/data/enemies.ts`)

```ts
/**
 * Tracks whether the player has made at least one Charge play this turn.
 * Reset to false at the start of every player turn.
 * Read by onPlayerNoCharge logic at end-of-player-turn.
 */
playerChargedThisTurn: boolean;
```

### `TurnState` additions (`src/services/turnManager.ts`)

The `chainMultiplierOverride` field must be read from the active enemy template and applied in the chain damage calculation path. No new fields required on `TurnState` — the override is read directly from `activeEnemy.template.chainMultiplierOverride` during chain multiplier resolution.

## Variant System

Each of the 12 base behaviors maps to 2-3 visual variants using the 88 existing sprites. Variants share their base's behavior, intent pool, and callback functions. They differ only in: sprite key, display name, HP ±10-15%, and damage ±10-15%.

**Naming convention:** Variants are defined as separate `EnemyTemplate` entries with `variantOf: 'base_enemy_id'` (new optional field on `EnemyTemplate`). The `variantOf` field is informational — it does not change runtime behavior.

### Example Variants

| Base | Variant Name | HP Change | Damage Change | Notes |
|------|-------------|-----------|---------------|-------|
| Cave Bat | Cave Bat Alpha | +15% HP | +10% dmg | Same behavior, larger/redder sprite |
| Cave Bat | Dusk Bat | base | base | Act 2 reskin using a darker sprite |
| Crystal Golem | Iron Golem | +10% HP | base | Brownish sprite variant |
| Toxic Spore | Poison Bloom | base | +15% dmg | Different floral sprite |
| Shadow Mimic | Dark Shade | +10% HP | base | Deeper shadow sprite |
| Bone Collector | Grave Warden | +12% HP | +10% dmg | Armored skeletal sprite |
| The Scholar | Lore Keeper | +15% HP | base | Robed figure sprite, Act 3 common pool |

Total target: 24-36 variants from 12 base behaviors, consuming the 88-sprite inventory without waste.

### Step 5 — Wire Callbacks Into `turnManager.ts` (`src/services/turnManager.ts`)
Three integration points:

**5a — After Charge resolves wrong:** In `playCardAction`, after the wrong-Charge branch executes, check `activeEnemy.template.onPlayerChargeWrong` and call it with `EnemyReactContext`. Apply any returned enemy HP changes to the instance.

**5b — After Charge resolves correct:** In `playCardAction`, after the correct-Charge branch executes, check `activeEnemy.template.onPlayerChargeCorrect` and call it with `EnemyReactContext`.

**5c — At end of player turn:** In the end-of-player-turn logic, if `activeEnemy.template.onPlayerNoCharge` exists and `!enemyInstance.playerChargedThisTurn`, call it. Reset `playerChargedThisTurn = false` for next turn start.

**5d — Reset `playerChargedThisTurn`:** At the start of each player turn, set `enemyInstance.playerChargedThisTurn = false`.

**5e — Set `playerChargedThisTurn`:** When any Charge play is initiated (before quiz fires), set `enemyInstance.playerChargedThisTurn = true`.

**Acceptance criteria:**
- `shadow_mimic` callback fires exactly once per wrong Charge
- `the_examiner` accumulates Strength correctly across turns where no Charge is played
- `the_scholar` heals correctly on correct Charges
- `playerChargedThisTurn` resets between turns
- No callback fires during Quick Play (only on Charge paths)

## Notes and Constraints

- The `onPlayerChargeWrong`, `onPlayerNoCharge`, and `onPlayerChargeCorrect` callbacks must return void and mutate state via the `EnemyReactContext` reference. They must NOT call Svelte store setters directly — all state updates flow through `turnManager.ts` after the callback returns.
- The `quizPhases` field on `EnemyTemplate` is defined here but the runtime implementation lives in AR-59.7. This AR only adds the field and populates the arrays with correct data; it does not implement the quiz phase interrupt logic.
- Do NOT delete old region-based enemy templates (`ore_wyrm`, `fossil_guardian`, etc.) in this AR. Deprecate only. Deletion is AR-59.19 scope.
- The Examiner's Strength stacks should use `turns: 999` (effectively permanent for the encounter duration). Using `turns: 99` is also acceptable — pick whichever is the project convention for "encounter-permanent" status effects.
- The Librarian's `quickPlayImmune` flag must be checked AFTER calculating Quick Play damage but BEFORE applying it to `enemy.currentHP`. The zero-damage result should still trigger hit animations (enemy flashes) to communicate the immunity visually — do not suppress the animation.
