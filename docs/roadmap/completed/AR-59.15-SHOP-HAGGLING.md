# AR-59.15 ‚Äî Shop Haggling

## Overview

**Goal:** Add an optional haggling mechanic to every shop purchase. Before completing any buy, the player can answer 1 quiz question for a 30% discount. Wrong answer = full price with no penalty. Haggling is always optional. Also introduces v2 shop pricing (rarity-based cards, revised relics) and a new Card Removal service.

**Dependencies:**
- AR-59.1 ‚Äî Charge/quiz infrastructure (provides `QuizOverlay` and `updateReviewStateByButton`)
- AR-59.5 ‚Äî Three-act run structure (shops exist in acts before this can be playable end-to-end)

**Estimated complexity:** Small-Medium ‚Äî purchase modal + quiz flow + pricing constants + removal service

---

## Design Decisions

- **D13:** Gold economy is active (~400-800g per run). All prices below are calibrated against this budget.
- **D21:** Card removal starts at 50g (down from no service previously), +25g per removal in the same run. Haggling applies to removal cost.
- Haggling is **always optional** ‚Äî "Buy (Xg)" always works without a quiz.
- **One haggle attempt per item per visit.** After a wrong answer, the Haggle button is disabled for that item; the player can still pay full price or cancel.
- FSRS is updated for every haggle question regardless of outcome (`'again'` for wrong, `'okay'` for correct).
- Floor discount (`SHOP_FLOOR_DISCOUNT_PER_FLOOR`) is kept for now but does **not** stack with haggling ‚Äî haggling IS the in-visit discount mechanic.

## v2 Shop Pricing

### Card Prices (rarity-based ‚Äî replaces current tier-based)

Cards do not have a `rarity` field; map tier ‚Üí rarity for pricing:
- Tier 1 = `common`
- Tier 2a / 2b = `uncommon`
- Tier 3 = `rare`

| Rarity | Base Price | Haggled (30% off) |
|--------|------------|-------------------|
| Common | 50g | 35g |
| Uncommon | 80g | 56g |
| Rare | 140g | 98g |

### Relic Prices

| Rarity | Base Price | Haggled (30% off) |
|--------|------------|-------------------|
| Common | 100g | 70g |
| Uncommon | 160g | 112g |
| Rare | 250g | 175g |
| Legendary | 400g | 280g |

### Card Removal Pricing

- **Base:** 50g for first removal in run
- **Escalation:** +25g per removal already performed in the run
  - 1st removal: 50g (35g haggled)
  - 2nd removal: 75g (52g haggled)
  - 3rd removal: 100g (70g haggled)
- Tracked via `run.cardsRemovedAtShop` (new field on `RunState`)
- Blocked if deck size ‚â§ 5 cards

The v2 shop shows:
- **3 cards** ‚Äî random selection from the run pool, priced by rarity
- **1-2 relics** ‚Äî weighted random by rarity from eligible pool
- **Card Removal service** ‚Äî one row, price escalates per removal

Items the player cannot afford are shown greyed out (existing `.disabled` style).

## Haggling Flow

1. Player taps any buy button (card, relic, or Card Removal).
2. **Purchase modal** appears instead of an immediate purchase:
   - Shows: item name, description, current price
   - Buttons: **"Buy (Xg)"** | **"Haggle (quiz for 30% off)"** | **"Cancel"**
3. **"Buy"** path: purchase completes at full price. Modal closes.
4. **"Haggle"** path:
   a. 1 quiz question appears (inline within modal). Standard answer format, floor-scaled timer.
   b. **Correct:** price updates to `Math.floor(price * 0.7)`. "Haggled!" toast shown for ~800ms. Purchase auto-completes. FSRS updated with `'okay'`.
   c. **Wrong:** correct answer shown briefly (learning moment). Modal returns to item view at **full price**. Haggle button is now disabled (`haggledThisItem = true`). Player can still Buy at full price or Cancel. FSRS updated with `'again'`.
5. **"Cancel"** at any point: modal closes, no purchase.
