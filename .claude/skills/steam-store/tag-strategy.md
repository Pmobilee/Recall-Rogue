## Tag Notes

**"Education" tag — risk/reward:**
- Risk: Players searching "educational" expect Prodigy/Duolingo, not a roguelite. Wrong expectations = negative reviews.
- Reward: Zero competition in "educational + roguelite" intersection. Anyone searching for "games that actually teach you things" finds us immediately.
- Strategy: Include the tag but NEVER lead with "educational" in copy. Lead with "roguelite." Let the knowledge mechanic sell itself through gameplay, not category labels.

**"Relaxing" tag — justification:**
- No visible quiz timer (invisible internal only)
- No time pressure communicated to the player
- Turn-based, think-as-long-as-you-want
- Balatro uses this tag successfully despite being a roguelite
