## Option D — tight and punchy

## Notes

- Options A and D are the strongest. A has genre authority ("The knowledge roguelite" claims the niche like "The poker roguelike"). D is the most information-dense.
- "The dungeon doesn't grade on a curve" and "You get stronger the more you actually know" are the two best closing hooks — one has personality, one sells the core loop.
- None of these say "educational." The knowledge mechanic sells itself.
- The 3.5x number is the max chain multiplier (chain length 5). It's the most concrete power fantasy number in the game.
