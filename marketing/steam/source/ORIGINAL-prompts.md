# Marketing Assets

## Specs

These are for Steam and App Store listings.

| Asset | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio | Format |
|-------|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Steam Store Capsule | 460×215 | ~2:1 | PNG |
| Steam Store Header | 920×430 | ~2:1 | PNG |
| Steam Library Hero | 3840×1240 | ~3:1 | PNG |
| Steam Library Logo | 600×900 | 2:3 | PNG |

## System Prompt

```
You are generating marketing art for a pixel art card roguelite called Recall Rogue.

The game is about descending into an underground dungeon where real-world knowledge is your weapon. You answer trivia questions to power combat cards. The aesthetic is underground fantasy with knowledge/library undertones.

STYLE:
- Chunky pixel art, but slightly more polished for marketing — smoother composition, stronger focal points
- The game's color palette: warm cave browns, teal-cyan crystals, gold knowledge accents, purple arcane magic
- Include the game title "Recall Rogue" prominently (pixel art text style)
- Dynamic composition showing the game's core fantasy
```

---

### Steam Store Capsule (460×215)
> A wide banner showing a dark cave entrance with warm torchlight spilling out, pixel art combat cards fanning out from the cave mouth. The "Recall Rogue" title in pixel art font on the left side. Crystal formations and arcane rune glow accenting the cave edges. A small silhouette of an adventurer at the cave entrance. Rich dark background with warm highlights.

**Filename:** `steam-capsule.png`

### Steam Store Header (920×430)
> Wider version of the capsule concept. Dark underground cave scene with dramatic lighting — torches on the left, crystal glow on the right. Multiple combat cards arranged in a fan formation in the center foreground. "Recall Rogue" title prominently displayed upper-center in pixel art font. The cave transitions from shallow (warm browns) on the left to deep (cool blues/purples) on the right, showing the game's progression. Knowledge symbols (books, scrolls, constellations) subtly worked into the cave walls.

**Filename:** `steam-header.png`

### Steam Library Hero (3840×1240)
> Ultra-wide panoramic cave scene. Left side: the evening campsite with warm firelight, dungeon entrance archway. Center: the dungeon descent — stalactites, crystals, floating books, arcane runes carved into stone. Right side: the deep abyss — obsidian, magma veins, cosmic void glimpses. The entire horizontal journey of the game shown in one panoramic scene. No title text needed (Steam overlays the game logo). Rich detailed pixel art with atmospheric lighting transitions.

**Filename:** `steam-hero.png`

### Steam Library Logo (600×900)
> Tall portrait composition featuring the Recall Rogue logo/icon prominently centered. A vertical cave descent behind the logo — starting from warm torchlit stone at the top, transitioning through crystals in the middle, to void and stars at the bottom. The logo should be the clear focal point with the cave scene as backdrop.

**Filename:** `steam-logo.png`
